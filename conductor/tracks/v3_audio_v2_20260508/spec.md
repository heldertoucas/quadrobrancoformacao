# 🔊 Specification: Audio Atmosphere v2 (Immersive Sound)

## Overview
Develop a coherent auditory experience that adapts to the visual theme and provides physical weight to interface interactions.

## Functional Requirements
- **Theme-Specific Kits:** Provide different sets of sound effects for 8-bit (chiptune), Neon (synthwave), and Pro (clean).
- **UI Micro-interactions:** Add subtle "ticks" and "clicks" for menu opening, theme switching, and button hover.
- **Dedicated Volume Slider:** Add a persistent volume control within the Sound Drawer.
- **Reactive Visualization:** Make the text or background glow/vibrate slightly in sync with audio peaks.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Sound effects match the visual "mood" of the theme.
- [ ] Interface feels more "physical" through sound feedback.
- [ ] Volume can be adjusted independently of the OS.
