# 📲 Specification: QR Code & Sharing v2

## Overview
Integrate the QR Code organically into the application's premium aesthetic, removing the "broken" visual of the white box.

## Functional Requirements
- **Glassified QR:** Render QR dots directly onto a semi-transparent glass panel. Remove the solid white background.
- **Logo Integration:** Inject the program's icon (SVG) into the center of the QR code for brand reinforcement.
- **3D Card Flip Transition:** When activated, the main slide "flips" in 3D space to reveal the QR code on the back.
- **Cinema Mode:** One-click expansion to fill the entire viewport for large rooms.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] QR Code remains scannable even with glass transparency.
- [ ] Branding is clearly visible in the center.
- [ ] Transition from slide to QR feels like a physical interaction.
